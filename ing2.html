Program Overview

Degree

State Engineer (Ingénieur d'État en Informatique) — A highly respected elite professional track in Algeria.

Duration

5 Years total (2 Years Preparatory Cycle/Socle Commun + 3 Years Specialized Engineering Cycle).

Focus

Balancing rigorous mathematical foundations with highly practical software, network, and system engineering skills.

The Academic Journey

Years 1 - 2

Preparatory Cycle (Socle Commun)

Focuses on building standard scientific foundations. Key areas include Mathematical Analysis, Algebra, Basic Algorithmic Systems, Physics, and Logic Circuits.

Years 3 - 4

Core Engineering Base

Diving deep into core computer science principles. Advanced Algorithms, Operating Systems (OS), Graph Theory, Database Management Systems (DBMS), and Computer Networks.

Year 5

Specialization & Graduation Project

Advanced architectures, networking, or distributed systems capped off by the comprehensive PFE (Projet de Fin d'Études) thesis and industrial internship.

Specialized Module Breakdowns

A closer technical review at the key thematic courses that shape the specialized engineering years.

Advanced Computing

Artificial Intelligence & ML

Covers heuristic exploration methods, formal logic validation frameworks, and introductory statistical Machine Learning models. Students learn to handle complex decision trees, optimization rules, and adaptive systems design.

Security Infrastructure

Cybersecurity & Cryptography

Focuses on data safety protocols, authentication keys, symmetric/asymmetric encryption methods, and threat protection vectors. Includes operational practice designing perimeter security layers and mitigating vulnerability exploits.

Networking Core

Advanced Computer Networks

A rigorous review of modern communications. Covers the detailed routing mechanics of the TCP/IP suite, systematic CIDR subnet mapping, packet routing strategies, and structural configuration of physical and virtual switches.
